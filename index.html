Thank you for participating in the DefDroid experiment! For this experiment, we only collect data lightly. To be specific, when DefDroid discovers a potential naughty app and takes action to correct the app's misbehavior, it will log
  • the timestamp
  • app name
  • OS actions
  • a field explaining the reason for the action
    • We will collect this log and no other information. If you are an advanced user, you can examine the content of the log through ADB at /data/system/defense.log.
